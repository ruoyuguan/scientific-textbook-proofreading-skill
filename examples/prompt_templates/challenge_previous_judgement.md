# Prompt Template: Challenge Previous Judgement

Please re-check your previous judgement.

Focus on:

1. What convention did you assume?
2. What unit system did you assume?
3. What physical regime did you assume?
4. Can the original expression be correct under another convention?
5. Please rederive the formula from first principles.
6. State clearly whether your previous judgement should be retained, weakened, or corrected.

Do not defend the previous answer automatically. If it was wrong, say so plainly.
