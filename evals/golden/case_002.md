For a circular compact binary at leading quadrupole order, the strain amplitude does not scale simply as \(\mathcal{M}_c/r\). It includes the chirp mass to the \(5/3\) power and the gravitational-wave frequency to the \(2/3\) power, schematically

```tex
h \sim \frac{(G\mathcal{M}_c)^{5/3}}{c^4 D}(\pi f)^{2/3}.
```
