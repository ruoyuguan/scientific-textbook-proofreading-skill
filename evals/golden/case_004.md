The statement

```text
IceCube-Gen2 will increase the detector area to about 8 km^3.
```

contains a dimensional inconsistency.

An area must have dimensions of length squared, such as `km^2`. The unit `km^3` is a volume. Therefore, if the intended quantity is `8 km^3`, the text should refer to volume, not area.

A corrected statement is:

```text
IceCube-Gen2 will increase the instrumented volume to about 8 km^3.
```

If the surrounding discussion is about effective area, sensitivity, or aperture, that quantity should be stated separately with the appropriate units.
