The statement

```text
For a compact binary inspiral at leading order, the gravitational-wave frequency increases as the binary loses orbital energy to radiation reaction.
```

should not be flagged as an error.

At leading order, gravitational-wave emission removes orbital energy and angular momentum from the binary. For a quasi-circular inspiral, the orbital separation decreases and the orbital frequency increases. Since the dominant gravitational-wave frequency is twice the orbital frequency, the gravitational-wave frequency also increases.

Judgement: no correction needed.
